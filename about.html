About Me

I am a Mechatronics Engineer and PhD Researcher specializing in Vehicle Dynamics and Control Systems. My focus is on optimizing vehicle performance through system simulation and ride & handling analysis using Multibody Dynamics.

With a Master's degree in Vehicle Dynamics and a strong foundation in both academic and industry practices, I have honed my skills in MATLAB/Simulink, MSC ADAMS, SIMPACK, and Python to deliver innovative solutions that enhance vehicle control and system performance.

I am passionate about research and thrive in collaborative environments, consistently working towards delivering measurable results through analytical skills and practical strategies. My long-term goal is to contribute to the evolution of control systems for safer and more efficient vehicles.

Skills

  • Vehicle Dynamics & Ride Comfort Analysis
  • MATLAB / Simulink
  • Python for Simulation & Control
  • MSC ADAMS & SIMPACK (Multibody Dynamics)
  • Model Predictive Control (MPC), LQR, PID
  • Data Analysis & Visualization
