Resources

Curated resources including simulation templates, Python/MATLAB toolkits, literature, and reading lists to help researchers, students, and enthusiasts explore vehicle dynamics and control systems.

Simulation Toolkits

  • Vehicle Control Toolkit (Python) – Modular Python toolkit for control strategy implementation and simulation.
  • Quarter Car Model – Python/Simulink template for ride comfort analysis and suspension studies.
  • Bicycle Vehicle Model – 2-DOF pitch + vertical dynamics simulation for handling studies.

Literature & Reading

  • ScienceDirect – Research papers on vehicle dynamics and control systems.
  • IEEE Xplore – Conference papers and journals on automotive control.
  • SpringerLink – Vehicle dynamics and simulation textbooks.
  • MATLAB Central – Scripts, toolboxes, and examples for MATLAB/Simulink vehicle modeling.

Templates & Code Snippets

  • Python & MATLAB simulation templates for vehicle models – ready for modification and experimentation.
  • Control strategy templates: PID, LQR, MPC, Skyhook, Sliding Mode – plug-and-play ready.
  • Pre-built plots and visualization scripts for performance evaluation (ride comfort, pitch rate, control effort).
